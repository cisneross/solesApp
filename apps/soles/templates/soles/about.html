About Soles

Soles was created by skateboarder Sergio D. Cisneros.

More importantly, it is a platform/community for skateboarders to come and share personal experiences with a variety of skate shoes. It is also a great community resource to decide what pair of shoes you should cop next. Soles knows how integral of a role shoes play in our skateboarding careers, so whether you're looking for performance, style, or shoes somwhere in the middle, let our community help guide your decision.
